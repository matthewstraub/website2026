I'm a New York City-based Customer Success Manager at Meta with a passion for entrepreneurship, creativity, and problem-solving in the ever-changing landscape of AI and technology.

My background is anything but traditional — I created my own B.A. in Interdisciplinary Studies with a concentration in Critical Sound Design at Marymount Manhattan College, blending audio, music, and a strong liberal arts foundation into something uniquely my own.

Originally from a small town in Northeastern Pennsylvania, I bring small-town curiosity to big-city problems — always looking for the creative angle that others might miss.

Featured Project
Immersive Installation

remember

An immersive audio installation that presents an abstract simulation of dementia, using sound to pull participants into a forgetful world. Inspired by encounters with his grandmother experiencing dementia, the piece combines recorded family conversations with ambient recordings of elder care facilities and live cello.

Created as the culmination of studies at Marymount Manhattan College — a deeply personal work that bridges art, empathy, and technology.
